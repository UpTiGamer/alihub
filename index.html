Ali

Ali ibn Abi Talib was the cousin and son-in-law of the Prophet Muhammad. He is considered the first Imam in Shia Islam and the fourth Caliph in Sunni Islam.

Imam Hussein

Imam Hussein ibn Ali was the grandson of the Prophet Muhammad. He is highly revered in both Sunni and Shia Islam for his stand against oppression and injustice.

Imam Hassan

Imam Hassan ibn Ali was the eldest son of Ali and Fatimah, the grandson of the Prophet Muhammad. He is known for his wisdom and commitment to peace.
